Our Story

Where Words & Coffee Meet

Born from a love of dog-eared pages and perfectly pulled shots, Café Novella is more than a café; it's a sanctuary for the curious, the dreamers, and the quietly passionate.

☕📖

The Beginning

A Love Letter to Reading

Café Novella was born in 2024 from a simple idea: what if the best coffee in the city lived inside the best bookshop in the city? Not side by side, but genuinely woven together.

We believe every great cup of coffee deserves a great story alongside it. Our shelves are curated by readers, our beans sourced from farms we've visited, and our space designed to slow you down in the best possible way.

Every table has a small recommendation card. Every drink has a paired book. Every visit is a new chapter.
